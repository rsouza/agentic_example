Barcelona, 12 March 1902

Dear Señor Vidal,

I am writing to inform you that the Biblioteca de Catalunya has agreed to accept the donation of the correspondence you mentioned during your visit last autumn. The librarian, Miquel Font, asked me to convey his gratitude and to confirm that the papers will be catalogued under the name of the Vidal family.

I visited the reading room on Tuesday and found three letters from your grandfather, Ramon Vidal, addressed to the publisher Eduard Domènech in Girona. They are dated between 1878 and 1881 and concern the preparation of the second edition of the almanac. The condition of the papers is good, though the ink on the earliest letter has faded slightly at the margins.

Please let me know whether you wish to retain copies before the originals are transferred. I expect to be in Barcelona until the end of April and could arrange a time for you to visit if you prefer to inspect them in person.

With my warmest regards,
A. Castelló
