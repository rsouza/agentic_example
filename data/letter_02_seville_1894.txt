Seville, 18 June 1894

Esteemed colleague,

The printer in Seville announced a new edition of poems by Antonio Ruiz and asked readers to send subscriptions. The notice was meant to reach both bookshops and local reading circles, since the editor believed the edition might sell quickly.

Please advise whether the notice should also be sent to the archive copy desk. I have also enclosed a short list of the proofs that still require attention before the issue goes out.

Yours sincerely,
Editor
